Summary
This paper deals with the extension of the Apriori algorithm and of the association
rules to the symbolic histogram and interval-valued data. We suggest a method that
will enable us to discover rules at the level of the concepts. This extension requires new
definitions for the support and the confidence in order to take advantage of the sym-
bolic structure of the data. The market basket data example is developed throughout
the paper. Thus, instead of mining rules between different items of some transactions
recorded in a retail organization like in the classical case, we discover rules at the level
of the customers in order to study their purchase behavior.
